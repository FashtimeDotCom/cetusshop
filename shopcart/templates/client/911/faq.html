1. How we charge freight cost?

There are many different ways to ship 911Signal goods to customers, it could be by express courier service if you didn't order much, by sea shipment if you there are a lot of goods in one shipment, and air cargo. In case of express delivery, we'll charge you upfront the freight cost, and make it clear on the invoice. The amount is calculated based on many previous actual packaging datas, so it's very reliable and accurate, therefore no need to worry if would overpay for express freight. Or if you have a courier account yourself, then you can inform it and in this case we'll not charge you again for the freight cost.

2. How about price list?

The price is negotiable. It can be changed according to your quantity or package. When you are making an inquiry, please let us know the quantity you want. There are different configuration on different products, it is better to provide us with your detailed requirements, so that we can offer a proper and best price.

3. What is your warranty policy?

We are very confident in our products, and we pack them very well, so usually you will receive your order in good condition. But due to long time shipment there will maybe some damage for the products. Any quality issue, we will deal with it immediately. Besides, it depends on the items, 2 years for lightheads & beacons, 1 year for lightbar & siren system.
