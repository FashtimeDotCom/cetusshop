Warrmth - THE FIREPLACE BRAND "Take Warrmth Home ..."

Warrmth is a dynamic, internationally-minded supplier with a strong vision based in China:

Our vision:

- To offer innovative, environmentally-focused products and bring Warrmth to every home.

Our belief:

- Innovation captures the market, reliability and flexibility holds on to it and this is our end focus.

We are inspired by Western styling and regularly work alongside overseas designers to provide a more novel and focused product base to suit the application or requirement of our clients.

The Company specializes in indoor decorative fireplaces marketed under our Warrmth brand – this includes wooden mantelpieces, electric fireplaces (including wall-mounted, recreational, classical and portable styles), bio–ethanol (gel) fireplaces, gas logs, accessories and so forth. The products we produce are pleasing to the eye, suitable, easy to use and energy efficient and hold European certificates such as SATIC, TUV, CE/GS, Reach, CB, and cETLul, or CSA.

Warrmth is a modern business with more than a decade of experience and loyal staff who have been with us from the beginning. We were one of the first Chinese companies to export bio-ethanol (gel) fireplaces, which we began to manufacture in 2003. We offer OEM and ODM - integrating development, manufacturing with marketing departments to achieve the desired results. We have our own specialized technicians, engineers, QC/QA who ensure the products are designed, developed and produced to the required standards.

Since safety has always been primary concern of ours, in 2004 we started cooperating with our European buyers to improve our products’ security features. Warrmth was the first Chinese supplier to hold the SITAC safety certificate, which all our bio-ethanol (gel) fireplaces hold. What’s more, we have passed more than 10 years without receiving a single quality complaint.

The in-house manufacturing capability for wooden mantelpieces is 400 per day, and guaranteed to be produced to both required quality and delivery time.

Being a company which is small, dynamic and by treating clients with a detailed, tailored service we always aim to meet requirements and expectations.

In order to maintain the highest ideals, all products comply with recognized international standards and are inspected at each production stage - a process that secures our success and growth. The company has worked for and holds numerous quality certificates and operates with a history of very low rejects which keeps our manufacturing costs low.

Building on our past success Warrmth is now successfully competing throughout Europe and North America and is looking for like-minded clients to work with to develop strong partnerships.

We look forward to your inquiry and the start of a long-term, mutually beneficial relationship.

"Our Warrmth penetrates our clients, reaching into their customers’ homes"
